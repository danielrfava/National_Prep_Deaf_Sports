Policy

Terms of Use

These terms govern access to NPDS, school-linked submissions, and general platform use.

Platform Use

National Prep Deaf Sports is a review-based publishing and preservation platform. Use of the platform means accepting these operating rules.

Authorized Use Only

Users submitting school data should be authorized school staff, approved helpers, or another legitimate school-associated contact with permission to provide records.

No Misuse

Impersonation, spam, harassment, abuse of school identity, false submissions, bulk abuse, or attempts to bypass review controls are not allowed.

Review and Moderation

NPDS may review, reject, request clarification on, edit for consistency, delay, archive, or remove submissions when needed to protect platform trust or record quality.

Account Control

Accounts may be limited, rejected, archived, or revoked when authorization is unclear, misuse is suspected, or school-linked access is no longer appropriate.

Submissions and Approval

Submission does not guarantee publication. NPDS may require additional verification, source support, or school confirmation before data appears publicly.

Platform Availability

NPDS may change platform features, workflows, or submission requirements over time as the system grows.
