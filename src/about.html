About NPDS

Built for today's stats. Designed to rescue yesterday's history. Mission, trust, and policy live here in one place.

Platform Trust

Everything below stays inside About so staff, schools, and public users can quickly understand how NPDS operates.

NPDS is an independent preservation and publishing platform for Deaf high school sports history. It supports visibility, recovery, and review. It does not replace school authority, NDIAA authority, or official school recordkeeping.

Mission

National Prep Deaf Sports preserves and publishes Deaf high school athletic records across the country so athlete legacies, school history, and program milestones remain visible over time.

That means supporting both present-day submission workflows and historical recovery work that many schools have never had time or staff to organize digitally.

Independent Role

NPDS exists to document, review, and present data in a searchable form. It is not a governing body and it is not presented as a replacement for school, coach, athletic director, or association authority.

Schools remain the most important source for correction requests, confirmation, and long-term record stewardship.

Submission Philosophy

Submissions are voluntary and review-based. Real school staff can help preserve stats, schedules, and history, but public trust matters more than speed, so NPDS keeps intentional approval friction in place.

What NPDS Publishes

Approved athlete, school, season, and record data may appear on public pages, search views, and historical research surfaces once reviewed and accepted into the platform.

Information Collected

NPDS may collect account details, school-affiliation details, submission metadata, uploaded files, parsed records, and contact information provided through school access requests or platform communication.

How It Is Used

This information is used to review school account requests, evaluate submission legitimacy, trace who submitted data, manage approvals, and improve record quality over time.

Public Records Visibility

Approved athletic records, athlete names, school names, seasons, and historical stats may appear publicly on the platform once they are reviewed and accepted for publication.

Privacy Requests

Questions about account, submission, or contact-data handling should be directed to NPDS through the platform's official contact path. Sensitive school-account issues may require identity verification before action is taken.

Authorized Use Only

Users submitting school data should be authorized school staff, approved helpers, or another legitimate school-associated contact with permission to provide records.

No Misuse

Impersonation, spam, harassment, abuse of school identity, false submissions, or attempts to bypass review controls are not allowed.

Review and Moderation

NPDS may review, reject, request clarification on, edit for consistency, or remove submissions when needed to protect record quality or platform trust.

Account Control

Accounts may be limited, rejected, archived, or revoked when authorization is unclear, misuse is suspected, or school-linked access is no longer appropriate.

Accuracy

NPDS works to preserve and present records carefully, but it does not guarantee that every stat, ranking, or historical entry is perfectly complete at all times.

Ongoing Corrections

Records may be corrected, expanded, or updated over time as better source material, school confirmation, or new archival evidence becomes available.

Correction Requests

Official correction requests should come from school staff, an Athletic Director, or another authorized school contact whenever possible so changes can be reviewed with proper context.

Public Questions

Public users who notice a possible error should contact the school or NPDS and provide as much supporting detail as possible, rather than assuming the first visible number is final forever.

NPDS Platform Materials

The National Prep Deaf Sports name, branding, original written content, and overall platform presentation are protected and should not be copied or republished as if they were an unrelated commercial product.

School and Third-Party Marks

School names, logos, mascots, and other third-party marks remain the property of their respective schools, organizations, or rights holders.

Commercial Reuse

The compiled presentation, structure, and branding of NPDS should not be reused commercially without permission, even when underlying sports facts may originate from public or school-provided sources.

Respect for Sources

NPDS may reference school-provided records, archived publications, and other historical material, but source context and ownership should still be respected when content is quoted, republished, or redistributed.
