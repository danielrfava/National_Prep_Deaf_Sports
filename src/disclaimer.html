Policy

Data Accuracy Disclaimer

NPDS works to preserve records carefully, but historical sports data is often incomplete, inconsistent, or evolving.

Accuracy and Source Reality

National Prep Deaf Sports is built for long-term record stewardship, not perfection theater. Historical sports data often improves through repeated review.

Independent Platform Status

NPDS is an independent preservation and publishing platform. It is not presented as an official governing authority and does not replace school or association recordkeeping.

Source Reality

NPDS may use school-provided records, structured submissions, archived publications, schedules, historical summaries, yearbooks, and other source material. Some historical datasets are incomplete or non-standard by nature.

Accuracy Goal

NPDS aims for the highest practical level of accuracy and consistency. That goal is ongoing and depends on source quality, school participation, and continued correction work.

No Perfection Guarantee

NPDS does not guarantee that every stat, ranking, season entry, or historical record is perfectly complete at all times.

Ongoing Corrections

Records may be corrected, expanded, replaced, or updated when stronger source material, school confirmation, or better historical evidence becomes available.

Public Questions and Corrections

Users who notice a possible error should contact NPDS and provide supporting detail whenever possible rather than assuming the first visible number is final forever.
