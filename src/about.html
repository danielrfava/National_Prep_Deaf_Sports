About NPDS

Built for today's stats. Designed to rescue yesterday's history.

Mission

National Prep Deaf Sports preserves and publishes Deaf high school athletic records across the country.

NPDS supports modern submission workflows and long-term historical recovery so school legacies remain visible.

What NPDS Tracks

Athletes, schools, seasons, and records from current and historical data sources.

Research views surface raw stats. Submission tools feed future approved records.
