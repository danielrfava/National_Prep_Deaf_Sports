Privacy Policy

This page explains what NPDS may collect, how platform information is used, and how school-related submissions are handled.

Privacy Overview

National Prep Deaf Sports uses school-linked workflows, submission review, and historical preservation tools that may involve account, contact, and evidence data.

Information We May Collect

NPDS may collect account details, school-affiliation details, submission metadata, uploaded files, parsed records, contact information, and communication submitted through access requests, correction requests, or school-related workflows.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to review school account requests, evaluate submission legitimacy, process uploads, manage approvals, improve record quality, respond to correction requests, and maintain platform operations.

Public Records Visibility

Approved athletic records, athlete names, school names, seasons, and historical stats may appear publicly on the platform after review and acceptance for publication.

Uploaded Files and Submission Evidence

Files uploaded to NPDS may be stored for review, validation, source tracking, moderation, correction handling, and historical verification. Evidence files are not automatically treated as public records without review.

Contact and School-Linked Requests

Questions involving account access, school-linked submissions, or contact-data handling should go through NPDS's official contact path. Sensitive school-account issues may require identity verification before action is taken.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as platform workflows evolve. Continued use of the platform after updates means the current posted version governs.
