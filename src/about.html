About

National Prep Deaf Sports

Preserving and publishing Deaf high school athletic history.

Platform Overview

National Prep Deaf Sports is an independent platform built to organize athlete records, school history, season results, and long-term statistical preservation across generations of Deaf sports.

NPDS supports both modern school submissions and historical recovery work. It is designed to improve visibility, continuity, and record stewardship over time.

Mission

National Prep Deaf Sports exists to preserve Deaf high school athletic records so athlete legacies, school history, and program milestones remain visible across generations.

Independent Role

NPDS is an independent publishing and preservation platform. It is not a governing body and does not replace school authority, athletic director authority, or association recordkeeping.

Submission Philosophy

Submissions are review-based. Schools and authorized contacts help improve trust, fill gaps, and strengthen long-term record quality.

Corrections & Stewardship

Records may be updated as better source material, school confirmation, or archival evidence becomes available. Accuracy improves over time through review and correction.
